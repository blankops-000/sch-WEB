About Outering School

Knowledge is Precious - Our Guiding Motto

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by the visionary Charles Jb as a non-formal education centre, formally registered as a learning institute in 2007. Our school has a rich heritage rooted in excellence and a deep commitment to education.

Over the years, we have evolved from a single institution into a dynamic and forward-thinking educational center that nurtures young minds to thrive in an ever-changing world.

In our journey of growth, the school transitioned to multi-levels: Pre Primary, Primary, Junior School, High School, and now Senior School with Grade 10 starting in January 2026.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

We nurture every learner's potential.

Vision

To provide quality education at affordable cost and to create centres of excellence in which each child will develop: the highest possible moral, academic, cultural and sporting standards; a wide range of interests, capacities and talents; a strong sense of social responsibility and carry these values into adult life.

Our Aims

  • To offer the Kenyan curriculum drawing on best practices
  • Prove that any child, irrespective of background, has the potential to rise and become the best both in academic and co-curriculum activities
  • To become a world-class school
